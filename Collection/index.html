My Tetris™ Collection

CTWC - Classic Tetris World Championships

During the CTWC (Classic Tetris World Championship) 2024 in Pasadena (CA), I had the opportunity to meet the creator of Tetris, Alexey Pajitnov, and Henk Rogers, the person who brought Tetris to the Famicom (BPS), The NES (Nintendo) and the GAME BOY (Nintendo). Later they founded the Tetris Company who now own the sole rights to Tetris. On the image is missing Maya Rogers, the daughter of Henk, who is now CEO of the Tetris Company. All three signed my copies of Tetrises.
