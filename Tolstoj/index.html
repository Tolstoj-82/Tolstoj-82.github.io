My Tetris journey

1991 - Game Boy

My Tetris journey could have started in 1991, when us kids were gifted a Game Boy for Christmas. The two games that we received alongside the console were Tetris and Super Mario Land. I found Tetris to be boring and avoided it. My older brother and my father, however fell in love with the game and played it often.

The early 2000s

Years passed and I discovered Tetris every now and again and played it aimlessly for a while.
